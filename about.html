About Us

Brijesh Banger Memorial Hospital (BBMH) is a prominent multi-super specialty hospital located in R.C. Vyas Colony, Bhilwara, Rajasthan. Established as a tertiary care center, BBMH offers comprehensive medical services across various specialties, including Neurology, Gynecology and Obstetrics, General Surgery, General Medicine, ENT, and Ophthalmology. The hospital is equipped with 198 beds, 60 of which are designated for intensive care units (ICUs), ensuring advanced healthcare infrastructure for critical and general medical needs. BBMH is recognized for its patient-centric approach, competent medical care, and a range of services such as Path Lab, CT Scan, X-Ray, and an in-house Pharmacy. The hospital operates outpatient departments (OPD) from Monday to Saturday, with timings from 10:00 AM to 2:00 PM and 4:00 PM to 6:00 PM. For appointments or emergency services, individuals can contact the hospital at 01482-297501.
